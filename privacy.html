Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-01-31

Data Collection

What we collect:

  • Game events (kills, deaths, assists) via Overwolf GEP
  • Video recordings via Overwolf Replays API
  • Real-time screen captures to assist Overwolf GEP events for precise highlight timing and post-game vertical video editing (stored locally, not used for tracking abilities or cooldowns)
  • Local app settings
  • In-game overlay for recording status notifications (hotkey reminders only, does not display any game data or competitive advantage information, no ads)

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Riot account credentials
  • Enemy ability cooldowns or ultimate timers
  • Any data that provides competitive advantages
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your computer. We have no servers and do not upload any data.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You can delete recorded clips anytime. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
